Able Player Video Demo #7:
Video player with manually coded external transcript

The data-transcript-src attribute is used to identify the id of a container that contains a pre-existing manually coded transcript. Manually-created transcripts can include better overall structure than automated ones, and can include additional HTML markup beyond the simple markup that is generated automatically by Able Player. This feature therefore gives authors greater control over the content of their transcripts.

For additional demos see the Index of Able Player Examples.
